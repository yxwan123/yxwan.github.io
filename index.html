Biography

I am a first year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), starting from Aug. 2023. I feel lucky to be advised by Prof. Michael R. Lyu . I received my B.E. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, in 2023.

Research Interests

I am interested in the application of multi-modal models in software engineering tasks. Currently I am working on a project related to UI to code generation. I am also interested in the reliability and safety of large language models and multi-modal models.
